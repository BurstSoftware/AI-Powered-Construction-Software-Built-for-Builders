## Overview

Burst automates the everyday work of contractors – from estimating to invoicing – so you win more jobs, save time, and boost profitability.

It is a complete construction management platform designed specifically for residential contractors, remodelers, and small-to-mid-size general contractors. The platform connects estimating, budgeting, invoicing, payments, change orders, daily logs, time tracking, and financial reporting in one seamless system.

## Core Solutions / Modules

- **Estimates** – Create and send professional estimates with ease
- **Budgets** – Effortless budgets with cashflow tracking
- **Invoices** – All-in-one invoicing tool
- **Bills** – Seamlessly connect invoicing and budgeting
- **Payments** – Pay and get paid with one click
- **Change Orders** – Efficient management of project changes
- **Daily Logs** – Photo-based daily reporting with weather data
- **Time Tracking** – Track time per employee, job, and line item
- **Reporting** – Construction financial reporting and insights
- **Staff Management** – Role-based access and permissions
- **Subcontractor Management** – Streamlined subcontractor workflows
- **Cost Tracking** – Real-time project cost monitoring

## Estimating

Great estimates win profitable work and keep jobs on track. Burst turns institutional knowledge into a fast, repeatable system.

**Key Capabilities:**
- AI-powered estimating: Describe project or upload plans → generate complete line-item estimate in minutes
- Reusable templates with industry standard codes
- Standardize markup, overhead, and target margins
- Automatic conversion from Estimate → Budget of Record
- Change orders and progress billing tied directly to original estimate
- Client sharing with digital approvals and branding

## Budgeting & Cash Flow
